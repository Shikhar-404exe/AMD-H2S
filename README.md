# CivicMind  
### AI-Powered Civic Engagement Platform

## 🌍 About CivicMind

CivicMind is a smarter way for citizens and city administrators to work together.

Instead of just collecting complaints, CivicMind understands them.

Built for **AMD Hackathon 2026**, this platform uses AI and real-time data to help cities respond faster, prioritize better, and even predict problems before they escalate.

Our goal is simple:  
**Make cities more responsive, more intelligent, and more human-centered.**

---

## 🚨 The Problem We’re Solving

Traditional civic reporting systems often fail because:

- Citizens report issues, but don’t see progress.
- Everything is marked “urgent,” so nothing truly is.
- Environmental and health data are ignored.
- Administrators face unstructured, overwhelming data.
- There is no predictive intelligence — only reactive action.

Cities deserve systems that think, not just store data.

---

## 💡 Our Approach

CivicMind transforms issue reporting into a proactive civic intelligence ecosystem.

When a user reports a problem, the system:

- Automatically categorizes it using AI  
- Evaluates real-time environmental conditions  
- Calculates a smart priority score  
- Considers crowd density and public health impact  
- Suggests wellness-safe areas if stress levels are high  

Instead of asking “Who reported first?”, we ask:  
**“What matters most right now?”**

---

## ✨ Key Features

### 🧠 Smart Issue Reporting

- Capture photos instantly  
- AI auto-categorizes issues (9 civic categories)  
- GPS-based location tracking  
- Real-time priority scoring  

Priority levels:
- **Critical (70%+)**
- **High (50–70%)**
- **Medium (30–50%)**
- **Low (<30%)**

---

### ⚙️ AI Priority Engine

CivicMind calculates issue urgency using weighted intelligence: 
- Priority Score = (healthImpact × 0.4) +
(cognitiveLoad × 0.3) +
(crowdDensity × 0.2) +
(environmental × 0.1)

This ensures public health and safety concerns are handled first — not just the loudest complaints.

---

### 🌡 Real-Time IoT Integration

CivicMind connects with city sensors to understand context:

- Temperature monitoring  
- Air Quality Index (AQI) tracking  
- Noise level analysis  
- Historical data trends  
- Smart alerts and recommendations  

The system doesn’t just see the issue — it understands the environment around it.

---

### 🧘 Cognitive Load & Wellness Monitoring

Urban stress is real.

CivicMind tracks wellness indicators and:

- Suggests calm zones  
- Recommends safer, quieter routes  
- Visualizes stress trends over time  

It’s not just about fixing cities — it’s about supporting people.

---

### 📊 Admin Intelligence Dashboard

For city administrators, CivicMind provides:

- Issue distribution analytics  
- Multi-level filtering (Zone, Category, Priority, Status)  
- Smart prioritized queues  
- Resolution tracking  
- Data-driven governance tools  

No more guesswork. Just clarity.
